## Why Data Privacy Matters for Freelancers

Most freelancers sign NDAs. Many work with proprietary business information — unreleased product roadmaps, financial projections, internal strategy documents, personal client data. When you paste this into a cloud AI tool, you are — technically — sharing it with a third party.

Cloud AI providers typically claim they don't train on your paid-tier conversations, but the data still passes through their infrastructure. For truly sensitive work, "we promise not to use it" isn't the same as "it never left your machine."

Local AI gives you a simple, honest answer: the data never left your computer. No trust required.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can a local AI really replace ChatGPT for freelance work?**
For structured tasks — drafting client emails, summarizing documents, writing proposals, generating invoices — yes. Modern local models like Llama 3 and Mistral perform at or near GPT-4 level for these use cases. Where cloud AI still leads is open-ended creative brainstorming and tasks requiring current world knowledge.

**Q: What hardware do I need to run a local AI?**
Most laptops made in the last 3 years can run a capable local AI model. An 8GB RAM machine can run Llama 3 8B comfortably. 16GB or more enables larger, more capable models. Apple Silicon Macs (M1/M2/M3/M4) are particularly efficient for local AI workloads.
